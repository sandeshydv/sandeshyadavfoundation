Founder Message

Sandesh Yadav Foundation is dedicated to empowering students through quality educational guidance, career awareness, and equal learning opportunities. Founded with the vision of helping students make informed academic decisions, the foundation provides reliable information on CUET, Delhi University admissions, scholarships, career pathways, and higher education opportunities. Through digital initiatives, counseling support, and educational content, we strive to bridge the information gap and inspire students to achieve their goals with confidence and determination.

— Sandesh Yadav
Founder, Sandesh Yadav Foundation
